Areca Nut Disease Identification

Areca nut disease identification leverages machine learning to analyze images of areca nut plants, detecting diseases such as Bud Rot, Leaf Spot, and Stem Rot. Users can upload images, and the system provides a diagnosis along with relevant disease information for effective plant management.
