Split Disease Details

Disease Symptoms:

  • Yellowing: Premature yellowing of nuts when they are half to three-quarters mature
  • Splitting: Nuts splitting from either side or the tips, expanding longitudinally towards the calyx and exposing the kernel

Management

  • Improving drainage in areas with high water tables
  • praying Borax at a rate of 2g/l per palm during the early stages of the problem.
